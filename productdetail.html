Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the battery last?
The battery life depends on usage. With typical use (including notifications, workouts, and sleep tracking), the battery lasts up to 7 days. With heavy use (constant GPS tracking, always-on display), it lasts about 3-4 days.
Is the watch compatible with iPhone?
Yes, the watch is compatible with iPhones running iOS 12.0 or later. You can download the companion app from the App Store to sync data and manage settings.
Can I swim with this watch?
Yes, the watch has a 5ATM water resistance rating, which means it's suitable for swimming and can withstand water pressure up to 50 meters. However, we don't recommend using it for scuba diving or high-speed water sports.
How accurate is the heart rate monitor?
Our heart rate monitor uses advanced sensors and algorithms to provide medical-grade accuracy. In tests, it showed 98% accuracy compared to professional medical devices during various activities.
